Section 5: Reflective Section

A. WHAT

The project involved developing a secure web application emphasizing user authentication, encryption, and risk assessment. As part of the team, I contributed to identifying and mitigating vulnerabilities in early design stages and implementing secure coding practices.

B. SO WHAT

Working in a collaborative environment enhanced my understanding of risk management as per ISO 27005. Applying Kolb’s Experiential Learning Cycle (1984), I moved from conceptual understanding to active experimentation by implementing and testing security mechanisms. The iterative feedback improved both technical proficiency and team communication following Tuckman’s stages of team development (1965).

C. NOW WHAT

This experience reinforced my commitment to pursuing cybersecurity roles. I plan to further develop my expertise in threat modeling, ethical hacking, and risk governance. Integrating reflective practice into future projects will help sustain continuous professional growth.
